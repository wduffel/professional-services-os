## KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

### What's Missing in the Market:

1. **No unified platform** — Firms must buy separate systems for each service line
2. **Legacy UX** — Most systems are 5-10+ years old, dated interfaces
3. **Integration nightmare** — Systems don't talk to each other
4. **Pricing** — Per-seat pricing punishes growing firms
5. **API limitations** — Many lack robust APIs for custom integrations

### Our Opportunity:

| Gap | Our Solution |
|-----|--------------|
| Multiple systems | One platform, 4 verticals |
| Old UX | Modern React/Vue front-end |
| No API | API-first by design |
| Per-seat pricing | Flexible module pricing |
| Fragmented data | Unified client record across verticals |

## COMPETITIVE DIFFERENTIATION

The market needs a platform that:
- ✅ Serves legal + accounting + investments + insurance
- ✅ Has modern UX (most competitors are dated)

- ✅ Flexible pricing (not just per-seat)
