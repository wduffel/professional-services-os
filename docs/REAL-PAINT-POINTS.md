## LEGAL — Real Complaints Found

### Clio (Market Leader) — What Users Hate:

| Complaint | Quote | Root Problem | Our Solution |
|-----------|-------|--------------|--------------|
| **"Worst customer service"** | "FIVE calls later... I get an arrogant email" | Support is terrible | Build great support from day 1 |
| **"Can't cancel"** | "They require you to call... cancellation specialist never available" | Painful cancellation | Easy self-serve cancellation |
| **"Reporting is a joke"** | "They'll put in a 'feature request' for you. Ha!" | No reporting | Build real reporting |
| **"Not user friendly"** | "Clio is rubbish... reporting didn't work. Nothing worked." | UX is bad | Modern, intuitive UX |
| **"Migration nightmare"** | "We were lied to during our demo... migration was a nightmare" | Hard to switch TO | Build smooth import/migration |
| **"Expensive"** | "Clio is rubbish, they just have more money... to marketing" | Overpriced | Fair pricing |
| **"Can't do what they say"** | "Told us it could do things it cannot, and integrate with things it cannot" | False promises, bad demos | Accurate product |

### Alternative Frustrations:

- Caret: "awful and difficult to use", "terrible customer service"
- MyCase: Mentioned as alternative people want to escape TO
